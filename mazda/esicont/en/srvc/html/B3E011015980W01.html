DRIVE BELT AUTO TENSIONER INSPECTION [LF]

B3E011015980W01

1. Remove the drive belt. (See DRIVE BELT REPLACEMENT [LF].)

2. Verify that the generator drive belt auto tensioner moves smoothly in the operational direction.

• If it does not move smoothly, replace the generator drive belt auto tensioner.

3. Turn the generator drive belt auto tensioner pulley by hand and verify that it rotates smoothly.

• If it does not move smoothly, replace the generator drive belt auto tensioner.

4. Install the generator drive belt. (See DRIVE BELT REPLACEMENT [LF].)
