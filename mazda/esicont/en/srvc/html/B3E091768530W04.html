DASHBOARD CENTER PANEL REMOVAL/INSTALLATION

B3E091768530W04

1. Disconnect the negative battery cable.

2. Remove the decoration panel. (SeeDECORATION PANEL REMOVAL/INSTALLATION.)

3. Remove the screws.

4. Pull the dashboard center panel outward and detach clips A and locator pins B.

5. Disconnect the hazard warning switch connector.

6. Remove the dashboard center panel.

7. Install in the reverse order of removal.
