ACCELERATOR CABLE INSPECTION/ADJUSTMENT [ZJ, Z6]

B3E011341660W01

1. Verify that the throttle valve is fully closed.

2. Move the accelerator cable in the directions of A and B, and verify that the accelerator cable play is within the specification.

• If not within the specification, adjust the cable play using locknut C.
Standard
1.0-3.0 mm {0.04-0.11 in}
Tightening torque
9.8-14.7 N·m {100-149 kgf·cm, 86.8-130 in·lbf}
