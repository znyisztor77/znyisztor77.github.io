REAR COMBINATION LIGHT REMOVAL/INSTALLATION

B3E091851150W01

1. Disconnect the negative battery cable.

2. Remove the trunk end trim. (4SD) (See TRUNK END TRIM REMOVAL/INSTALLATION.)

3. Remove the fasteners and slightly bend back the trunk side trim. (4SD)

4. Remove the service hole cover. (5HB)

5. Remove in the order indicated in the table.

1
Nut
2
Connector
3
Rear combination light
4
Socket
5
Rear turn light bulb
6
Brake light/taillight bulb

6. Install in the reverse order of removal.
