BONNET LATCH SWITCH INSPECTION

B3E091467001W03

1. Open the bonnet.

2. Disconnect the negative battery cable.

3. Disconnect the bonnet latch switch connector.

4. Inspect for continuity between the bonnet latch switch terminals A and B.

• If there is continuity, replace the bonnet latch.

5. Lock the bonnet latch using a flathead screwdriver or equivalent as shown.

6. Inspect for continuity between the bonnet latch switch terminals A and B

• If there is no continuity, replace the bonnet latch switch.
Caution
• After the inspection, unlock the bonnet latch. If closing the bonnet with the bonnet latch locked, the bonnet latch and/or bonnet striker may be broken.
