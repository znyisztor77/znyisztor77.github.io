COOLANT RESERVE TANK REMOVAL/INSTALLATION

B3E011215201W01

Warning
• Remove and install all parts when the engine is cold, otherwise they can cause severe burns or serious injury.

1. Drain the engine coolant until the coolant reserve tank becomes empty. (See ENGINE COOLANT REPLACEMENT.)

2. Remove in the order indicated in the table.

.

1
Hose
2
Coolant reserve tank

3. Install in the reverse order of removal.

4. Add engine coolant. (See ENGINE COOLANT REPLACEMENT.)

5. Inspect for engine coolant leakage. (See ENGINE COOLANT LEAKAGE INSPECTION.)
