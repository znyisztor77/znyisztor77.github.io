B3E010201083W04

DTC P2503

Charging system voltage low

DETECTION CONDITION

• PCM needs more than 20 A from generator, and judges generator output voltage to be below 8.5 V during engine running.

POSSIBLE CAUSE

• Generator malfunction
• PCM and/or generator are poorly connected
• Open and/or short to GND in wiring from between generator terminal P and PCM terminal 2AM
• Open and/or short to GND in wiring from between generator terminal D and PCM terminal 2AQ
• Drive belt misadjustment
