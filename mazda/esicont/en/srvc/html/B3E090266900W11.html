DTC 03:ER01

B3E090266900W11

DTC 03:Er01

CD player

DETECTION CONDITION

• CD player cannot implement insert and eject commands.

POSSIBLE CAUSE

• Defective CD (broken or foreign material stuck/attached)
• CD player malfunction

STEP

INSPECTION

ACTION

1
Verify the condition of the CD when the error occurs.
Is the CD normal?
Yes
Go to the next step.
No
Repair (remove foreign material) or replace the CD, and go to the next step.
2
Clear the DTC.
Insert then eject a normal CD.
Is DTC 03: Er01 displayed?
Yes
Replace the CD player.
No
DTC troubleshooting completed.
