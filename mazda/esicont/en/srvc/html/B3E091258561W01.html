FRONT MANUAL WINDOW REGULATOR REMOVAL/INSTALLATION

B3E091258561W01

1. Disconnect the negative battery cable.

2. Remove the following parts:

(1) Inner garnish (See INNER GARNISH REMOVAL/INSTALLATION.)
(2) Regulator handle (See REGULATOR HANDLE REMOVAL.)
(3) Front door trim (See FRONT DOOR TRIM REMOVAL/INSTALLATION.)
(4) Front door speaker (See FRONT DOOR SPEAKER REMOVAL/INSTALLATION.)
(5) Front door glass (See FRONT DOOR GLASS REMOVAL/INSTALLATION.)

3. Remove the nuts and the bolts.

4. Remove the front manual window regulator through the speaker installation hole.

5. Install in the reverse order of removal.
