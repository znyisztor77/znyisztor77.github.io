B-PILLAR UPPER TRIM REMOVAL/INSTALLATION

B3E091768190W01

1. Remove the following parts:

(1) Upper anchor of the front seat belt (See FRONT SEAT BELT REMOVAL/INSTALLATION.)
(2) Front scuff plate (SeeFRONT SCUFF PLATE REMOVAL/INSTALLATION.)
(3) Rear scuff plate (SeeREAR SCUFF PLATE REMOVAL/INSTALLATION.)
(4) B-pillar lower trim (See B-PILLAR LOWER TRIM REMOVAL/INSTALLATION.)

2. Partially peel back the seaming welt.

3. Pull the B-pillar upper trim outward and detach clips A and locator pin B from the body.

4. Detach the tab from the body and remove the B-pillar trim.

5. Install in the reverse order of removal.
