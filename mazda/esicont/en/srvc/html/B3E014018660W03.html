2. Verify that the continuity between CPP switch terminals A and B is as indicated in the table.

• If there is no malfunction, perform the "Circuit Open/Short Inspection".

Circuit Open/Short Inspection

1. Disconnect the PCM connector.

2. Disconnect the CPP switch connector.

3. Inspect the following wiring harnesses for open or short circuit. (Continuity inspection)

Open circuit

• If there is no continuity in the following wiring harnesses, there is an open circuit. Repair or replace the wiring harness.
- CPP switch terminal B and PCM terminal 1O
- CPP switch terminal A and body GND

Short circuit

• If there is continuity in the following wiring harnesses, there is a short circuit. Repair or replace the wiring harness.
- CPP switch terminal B and body GND
- CPP switch terminal B and power supply
