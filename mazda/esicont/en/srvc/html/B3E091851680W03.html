FRONT FOG LIGHT BULB REMOVAL/INSTALLATION

B3E091851680W03

1. Disconnect the negative battery cable.

2. Slightly bend back the mudguard.

3. Disconnect the front fog light bulb connector and remove the front fog light bulb by turning it in the direction of the arrow.

Caution
• A halogen bulb generates extremely high heat when it is illuminated. If the surface of the bulb is soiled, excessive heat will build up and the life of the bulb will be shortened. When handling the bulb, hold the flange, not the glass.

4. Install in the reverse order of removal.
