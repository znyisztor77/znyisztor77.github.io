ABS HU/CM INSPECTION

B3E041343750W02

1. Disconnect the ABS HU/CM connector.

2. Connect the negative battery cable.

3. Attach the tester lead to the ABS HU/CM wiring harness-side connector, then inspect the voltage, continuity, or resistance according to the standard (reference) on the table.
