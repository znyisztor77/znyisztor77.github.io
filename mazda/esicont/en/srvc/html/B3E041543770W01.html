COMBINED SENSOR REMOVAL/INSTALLATION

B3E041543770W01

Caution
• The internal parts of the combined sensor could be damaged if dropped. Be careful not to drop the combined sensor. Replace the combined sensor if it is subjected to an impact. Also, do not use an impact wrench or other similar air tools when removing/installing the sensor.

1. Perform the following and partially peel back the floor covering.

(1) Remove the front seat (RH).
(2) Remove the inner scuff plate (RH).
(3) Remove the front side trim (RH).

2. Remove in the order indicated in the table.

1
Combined sensor connector
2
Bracket
3
Combined sensor

3. Install in the reverse order of removal.

4. After installation, perform the combined sensor initialization procedure. (See COMBINED SENSOR INITIALIZATION PROCEDURE.)
