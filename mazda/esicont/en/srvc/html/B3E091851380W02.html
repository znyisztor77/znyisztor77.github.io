INBOARD LIGHT BULB REMOVAL/INSTALLATION

B3E091851380W02

5HB

1. Disconnect the negative battery cable.

2. Remove the service hole cover.

3. Disconnect the inboard light bulb connector. (RH)

4. Disconnect the rear fog light bulb connector. (LH)

5. Remove the socket.

6. Remove the inboard light bulb. (RH)

7. Remove the rear fog light bulb. (LH)

8. Install in the reverse order of removal.
