CAR-NAVIGATION CONTROL SWITCH INSPECTION

B3E092066921W02

1. Operate the car-navigation control switch and verify that the car-navigation system operates normally.

• If the system does not operate normally, go to the next step.

2. Inspect the wiring harness between the LCD unit and the car-navigation control switch for an open circuit, power short, or a short to ground.

• If there is any malfunction in the wiring harnesses, repair or replace the applicable wiring harness.
• If the wiring harnesses are normal, replace the car-navigation control switch.
