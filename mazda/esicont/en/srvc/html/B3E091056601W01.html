BONNET REMOVAL/INSTALLATION

B3E091056601W01

Warning
• Removing the bonnet without proper support can be dangerous. The bonnet may fall and injure you. Always perform the following procedure with at least another person.

1. To remove the bonnet hinge, remove the following parts:

(1) Front side turn lights (See FRONT SIDE TURN LIGHT REMOVAL/INSTALLATION.)
(2) Front bumper (See FRONT BUMPER REMOVAL/INSTALLATION.)
(3) Front combination lights (See FRONT COMBINATION LIGHT REMOVAL/INSTALLATION.)
(4) Front fender panel (See FRONT FENDER PANEL REMOVAL/INSTALLATION.)

2. Disconnect the windshield washer hose.

3. Remove in the order indicated in the table.

1
Bonnet insulator
2
Shroud seal weatherstrip
3
Bonnet
4
Bonnet hinge
5
Bonnet stay

4. Install in the reverse order of removal.

5. Adjust the bonnet. (See BONNET ADJUSTMENT.)
