REAR POWER WINDOW REGULATOR REMOVAL/INSTALLATION

B3E091272560W01

1. Disconnect the negative battery cable.

2. Remove the following parts:

(1) Beltline molding (4SD) (See REAR BELTLINE MOLDING REMOVAL/INSTALLATION.)
(2) Sail garnish (5HB) (See SAIL GARNISH REMOVAL/INSTALLATION.)
(3) Rear door garnish (5HB) (See REAR DOOR GARNISH REMOVAL/INSTALLATION.)
(4) Rear door trim (See REAR DOOR TRIM REMOVAL/INSTALLATION.)
(5) Rear door speaker (See REAR DOOR SPEAKER REMOVAL/INSTALLATION.)
(6) Rear door glass (See REAR DOOR GLASS REMOVAL/INSTALLATION.)
(7) Rear power window motor (See POWER WINDOW MOTOR REMOVAL/INSTALLATION.)

3. Remove the nuts.

4. Insert your hand through the rear speaker installation hole, rotate the drum housing in the direction shown in the figure and disengage the tabs from the rear door module.

5. Remove the rear power window regulator through the speaker installation hole.

6. Install in the reverse order of removal.

Caution
• Make sure the cable does not unspool from the drum housing when installing.
