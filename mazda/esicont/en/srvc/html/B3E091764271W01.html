CONSOLE REMOVAL/INSTALLATION

B3E091764271W01

1. Detach clips A, B and C and remove the upper panel.

2. Detach clips D, E and F and remove the boot panel.

3. Remove screws A.

4. Detach clips G.

5. Disconnect the cigarette lighter connector and seat warmer switch connectors.

6. Remove the ashtray illumination, then remove the ashtray panel.

7. Remove the screws B.

8. Detach tabs H and remove the console.

9. Install in the reverse order of removal.
