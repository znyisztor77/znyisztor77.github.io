LOWER PANEL REMOVAL/INSTALLATION

B3E091764310W01

1. Disconnect the negative battery cable.

2. Detach the bonnet release lever from the lower panel. (SeeBONNET LATCH AND RELEASE LEVER REMOVAL/INSTALLATION [L.H.D.].) (SeeBONNET LATCH AND RELEASE LEVER REMOVAL/INSTALLATION [R.H.D.].)

3. Remove the front scuff plate. (Driver's side) (SeeFRONT SCUFF PLATE REMOVAL/INSTALLATION.)

4. Remove the front side trim. (Driver's side) (SeeFRONT SIDE TRIM REMOVAL/INSTALLATION.)

5. Remove the screw.

6. Pull the lower panel outward, and detach clips A and tab.

7. Disconnect the panel light control switch connector and the headlight leveling switch connector.

8. Remove the lower panel.

9. Install in the reverse order of removal.
