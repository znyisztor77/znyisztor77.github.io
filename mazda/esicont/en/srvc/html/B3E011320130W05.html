VARIABLE INTAKE-AIR SHUTTER VALVE ACTUATOR INSPECTION [LF]

B3E011320130W05

1. Remove the air hose. (See INTAKE-AIR SYSTEM REMOVAL/INSTALLATION [LF].)

2. Disconnect the vacuum hose from the variable intake-air shutter valve actuator.

3. Connect a vacuum pump to the variable intake-air shutter valve actuator.

4. Apply vacuum and verify that the rod moves.

• If the rod dose not move, replace the intake manifold.
