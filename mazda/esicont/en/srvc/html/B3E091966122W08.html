HEADLIGHT CLEANER ACTUATOR REMOVAL/INSTALLATION

B3E091966122W08

1. Disconnect the negative battery cable.

2. Remove the front side turn light. (See FRONT SIDE TURN LIGHT REMOVAL/INSTALLATION.)

3. Remove the front bumper. (See FRONT BUMPER REMOVAL/INSTALLATION.)

4. Remove the headlight cleaner nozzle cover.

5. Squeeze the clip tabs and slide the clip in the direction of the arrow.

6. Disconnect headlight cleaner hose from the headlight cleaner actuator.

7. Remove the screw, then remove the headlight cleaner actuator cover.

8. Remove the headlight cleaner actuator from the headlight cleaner actuator cover.

9. Install in the reverse order of removal.
