FRONT BUMPER DISASSEMBLY/ASSEMBLY

B3E091050010W02

Standard Type

1. Remove the following parts:

(1) Front fog lights (Vehicles with the front fog lights) (See FRONT FOG LIGHT REMOVAL/INSTALLATION.)
(2) Headlight cleaner hose (Vehicles with the headlight cleaner) (See HEADLIGHT CLEANER HOSE REMOVAL/INSTALLATION.)
(3) Headlight cleaner nozzles (Vehicles with the headlight cleaner) (See HEADLIGHT CLEANER NOZZLE REMOVAL.) (See HEADLIGHT CLEANER NOZZLE INSTALLATION.)
(4) Headlight cleaner actuator (Vehicles with the headlight cleaner) (See HEADLIGHT CLEANER ACTUATOR REMOVAL/INSTALLATION.)
(5) Radiator grille (4SD) (See RADIATOR GRILLE REMOVAL/INSTALLATION.)

2. Disassemble in the order indicated in the table.

1
Screw A
2
Set plate
3
Screw B
4
Front bumper mesh
5
Screw C (4SD)
6
Front fog light hole cover (vehicles without the front fog lights)
7
Screw D
8
License plate holder
9
Front bumper fascia

3. Assemble in the reverse order of disassembly.
