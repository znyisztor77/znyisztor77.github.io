DTC 00:Er01

Cassette deck

DETECTION CONDITION

• Cassette deck cannot implement insert and eject commands.

POSSIBLE CAUSE

• Defective cassette tape (deformation, broken, or foreign material stuck/attached)
• Cassette deck malfunction

Diagnostic procedure

STEP

INSPECTION

ACTION

1
Can cassette tape be ejected?
Yes
Go to the next step.
No
Replace the cassette deck.
2
Verify condition of the cassette tape when the error occurs.
Is the cassette tape normal?
Yes
Go to the next step.
No
Repair (remove foreign material) or replace the cassette tape, and go to the next step.
3
Clear the DTC.
Insert then eject a normal cassette tape.
Is DTC 00: Er01 displayed?
Yes
Replace the cassette deck.
No
DTC troubleshooting completed.
