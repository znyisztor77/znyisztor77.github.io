HAZARD WARNING SWITCH REMOVAL/INSTALLATION

B3E091866401W01

1. Disconnect the negative battery cable.

2. Remove the decoration panel. (See DECORATION PANEL REMOVAL/INSTALLATION.)

3. Remove the dashboard center panel. (See DASHBOARD CENTER PANEL REMOVAL/INSTALLATION.)

4. Disconnect the hazard warning switch connector.

5. Squeeze the tabs of hazard warning switch and pull it outward to remove it.

6. Install in the reverse order of removal.
